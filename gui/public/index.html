Safety Override

            

              Even if the GNN predicts a statement as dead, the
              hasSideEffect() check in
              analysis.cpp acts as a hard veto.
              Calls to printf, malloc,
              pointer-dereference assignments, and other side-effectful ops are never removed by the ML pass.
